Introductory Cases Studies

R

Case Study

Work group:

By group of 5 to 6 students:

  1. you will explore one of the following links
  2. you will search for the data type, qualification, etc
  3. you will question the workflow supporting the website
  4. you will question the interpretation, the vizualisation of the results, its strengths and weaknesses
  5. you will present your result using an RMarkdown presentation (HTML IoSlides or Beamer) - No more than 4 slides
  • First: names, title, link as subtitle
  • Second: survey of the data elements
  • Third: workflow 1
  • Fourth: critical analysis
